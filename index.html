About

Do you need your much loved motorcycle serviced, repaired, part's custom made or your bike picked up in a pinch. At Allpike we offer personalised, respectful honest service that can cater for your every need. I have 35 year's experience with motorcycle's from motocross, to road racing, enduro, cruisers and much more. If there's a part you just can't find, doesn't exist or you want it made to your specifications i can offer you that service.


We have a Lathe 600mm x 240 turning centre, Milling Machine 'Geared', AC/DC tig aluminium, stainless, mild steel and titanium. If it's on your bike and it's broken, worn out or not running we can certainly help!


At Allpike you will receive respect, one on one service and I'll work on your bike like it's my own. We specialise in Trail bike's, enduro, motocross and road and trail bikes. Road bikes more than welcome as well.
